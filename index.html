Virtual Riot

Producer. DJ. Preset Junkie

Virtual Riot, is a German DJ and electronic music producer. He has released seventeen extended plays and one studio album, most notably his 2016 EP Chemistry, which peaked at 20 on the Dance/Electronic Albums charts.
